ABOUT LOTUS 2025

Lotus marks the inaugural International Conference on Tourism, Culture, and Sustainability, organized by the undergraduate students of the Applied Bachelor Program in Tourism Destination Management at Bali Tourism Polytechnic. This pioneering initiative signifies a commitment to fostering critical dialogue and collaborative scholarship within the evolving landscape of sustainable tourism, while simultaneously cultivating leadership and organizational competencies among future tourism professionals.

Adopting the theme “Reimagining Wellness Destination Development: Integrating Nature, Culture, and Technology for Sustainable Destinations,” Lotus 2025 aspires to provide a dynamic platform for scholars, practitioners, policymakers, and students to exchange cutting-edge research, best practices, and innovative frameworks. The conference underscores the imperative of harmonizing environmental stewardship, cultural heritage preservation, and technological integration to advance the development of wellness destinations that are resilient, inclusive, and sustainable.

Lotus also convenes a distinguished lineup of experts in the fields of tourism, wellness, and sustainability, offering participants valuable insights and fresh perspectives to enrich their academic and professional pursuits. Through this initiative, Bali Tourism Polytechnic reaffirms its role as a catalyst for academic excellence and practical innovation in the tourism sector, contributing to the global discourse on sustainable development while nurturing a generation of professionals equipped to address the challenges and opportunities of the future tourism industry.
